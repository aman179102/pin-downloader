# Privacy Policy

**PinDownloader** ("the App") does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Handling

- **No analytics**: The App does not use any analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, or tracking frameworks.
- **No ads**: The App contains no advertisements of any kind.
- **No accounts**: The App does not require or implement user accounts or logins.
- **No backend**: The App operates entirely on-device. No data is sent to any external server controlled by the developer.
- **URL processing**: URLs you paste or share into the App are processed locally on your device solely to fetch downloadable media. These URLs are sent only to the third-party service you explicitly target (e.g., Pinterest).
- **Downloaded files**: Media files are saved to your device's local storage. The App does not upload, share, or transmit downloaded files.
- **Download history**: The App does not currently persist download history. If such a feature is added in the future, all history will remain on-device.

## Third-Party Services

When you provide a URL, the App makes a network request to the domain specified in that URL to retrieve public media content. This is the same type of request your browser would make.

## Your Responsibility

You are solely responsible for the content you download and share using this App. Only download content you own or have explicit permission to download.

## Changes

This privacy policy may be updated if the App's functionality changes. Users will be notified via the repository release notes.
